Nao

BOOTS (or Jordan Asher, his real name) is a singer, rapper, and producer who previously worked with Beyonce on her self-titled album in 2013, having a big hand in the production (most notably the song "Haunted"), before starting his solo work. His first solo song released was a song called "Dust", which was first shared on Beyonce's Facebook page in February 2014. He went on to release two more songs called "Howl" and "Autumn (Lude 1) featuring R&B singer Kelela. His debut mixtape "WinterSpringSummerFall" was released in May 2014, and his debut studio album "Aquaria" was released in November 2015. His music style is experimental hip-hop and R&B, with some elements of rock music.

Nao is an R&B/Soul singer-songwriter and record producer from East London. Her music career started off as a backup singer for other artists, then she joined a female a capella group called The Boxettes. Her first solo release was the So Good Ep released in October 2014. Her debut album For All We Know was released on July 29, 2016. She is known for her very light and sweet vocals, and describes her sound as "wonky-funk".
